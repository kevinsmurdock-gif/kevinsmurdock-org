What We Did

Five Commitments to South Carolina

Documented by BioSpace, WYFF, MarketWatch, Yahoo Finance, Business Insider, Upstate Business Journal, and the SC Governor's office.

🚒

Free COVID Testing for Every First Responder

Free COVID-19 testing for every law enforcement officer, firefighter, and EMT/paramedic in the state of South Carolina. No questions, no invoicing, no cap.

BioSpace · WYFF · MarketWatch · Yahoo News
😷

Thousands of N95 Masks Donated

25% of our entire mask supply — thousands of N95 respirators given to SC police, fire departments, and hospital systems during the earliest, scarcest months of the pandemic. 4 hospital systems and 11 first responder units received donations.

Yahoo Finance · Business Insider · Physician Family
💰

Years of Reinvestment Into Workforce and Community

Millions reinvested into expanding capacity, hiring workers who lost jobs during shutdowns, and serving communities that needed testing. No outside investors. Built from the ground up.

Upstate Business Journal · November 2020
👷

Hired Laid-Off Workers

When Greenville restaurants and local businesses shuttered, we personally committed to hiring the people who lost their jobs — bringing them into the lab operation, training them up, paying them while the rest of the economy froze.

Personal commitment · Documented hiring
🧬

$51M Investment, 185 New SC Jobs

In 2022, announced a $51 million capital investment creating 185 new jobs in Greenville County. Endorsed by the SC Secretary of Commerce. Announced at SCBIO 2022. Published on the SC Governor's official .gov website.

PRNewswire · SC Governor (.gov) · SCBIO 2022
🎓

University COVID Testing — Clemson & Furman

Became the preferred COVID testing provider for SC universities and their student-athletes, keeping students safely in the classroom and on the field through the hardest stretch of the pandemic.

Preferred provider agreements · 2020-2021
